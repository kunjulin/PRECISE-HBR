What is PRECISE-DAPT?

The PRECISE-DAPT (Predicting bleeding complications in patients undergoing stent implantation and subsequent Dual AntiPlatelet Therapy) score is a validated clinical decision support tool designed to assess bleeding risk in patients receiving dual antiplatelet therapy (DAPT) following percutaneous coronary intervention (PCI).

Clinical Problem

Dual antiplatelet therapy with aspirin and a P2Y12 inhibitor is the standard of care following PCI. However, determining optimal DAPT duration requires balancing:

  • Ischemic Risk: Shorter DAPT may increase risk of stent thrombosis
  • Bleeding Risk: Longer DAPT increases risk of major bleeding events

Clinical Solution

PRECISE-DAPT provides an evidence-based approach to personalize DAPT duration by:

  • Identifying patients at high bleeding risk who may benefit from shorter DAPT
  • Supporting standard DAPT duration in low-risk patients
  • Enabling informed shared decision-making
Clinical Impact: Studies show PRECISE-DAPT guided therapy can reduce bleeding events by 34% in high-risk patients without increasing ischemic events.

üìä Clinical Evidence & Validation

Derivation Study

  • Population: 14,963 patients from 8 randomized controlled trials
  • Primary Endpoint: TIMI major or minor bleeding at 1 year
  • C-statistic: 0.73 (excellent discriminative ability)
  • Calibration: Excellent across all risk categories

External Validation

  • Population: 8,595 patients from 3 additional randomized trials
  • C-statistic: 0.72 (maintained excellent performance)
  • Geographic Validation: Tested across multiple countries

Key Publication

Costa F, van Klaveren D, James S, et al. Derivation and validation of the predicting bleeding complications in patients undergoing stent implantation and subsequent dual antiplatelet therapy (PRECISE-DAPT) score. Lancet. 2017;389(10073):1025-1034.
